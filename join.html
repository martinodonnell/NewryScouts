Become a Scout

If you want to in role your child in scouts, fill out the application form found by clicking the image to the right, and bring it to the scouts on any of the following nights. If you want to ask any questions feel free to come down on any of the nights above or message on facebook. Click here to download an activity consent form.

Become a Leader

We are always looking for new leaders to help run our meetings. If you want to find out more, feel free to message us on our Facebook page or come down to the scout hall and talk to one of our leaders. We are a small but dedicated team that work together to help make sure all our scouts get the most out of scouting. And hey, it's only about two hours a week!
